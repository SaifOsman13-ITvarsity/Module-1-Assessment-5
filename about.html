ABOUT THE AVENGERS
Who are the Avengers?
The Avengers are a team of fictional superheroes and the protagonists of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) media franchise, based on the Marvel Comics team of the same name created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby in 1963. Founded by S.H.I.E.L.D. Director Nick Fury, the team is a United States-based organization composed primarily of superpowered and gifted individuals, described as "Earth's Mightiest Heroes", who are committed to the world's protection from a variety of threats.
After Endgame
After the events of Avengers: Endgame, heres what can be said about each member:
SPOILER ALERT!
  • Iron Man died at the end of Avengers: Endgame after using the Infinity Stones to snap Thanos and his army away for good. His body could not handle the immense gamma radiation caused by the snap.
  • Thor returns to New Asgard, where he makes Valkyrie the new ruler and leaves with the Guardians of the Galaxy into space. He is then later featured in Thor: Love and Thunder.
  • During Endgame Bruce managed to merge his two identities. He now has the physical form of The Hulk and the mind of Bruce Banner. After the movie he is seen in the show "She-Hulk Attorney at Law" where he mentors his cousin who seems to have similar powers to himself.
  • Captain America return all the Infinity Stones to their original timelines and then instead of returning to his own, chooses to stay in the past with his lost love, Peggy Carter. Therefore in the present, he appeares aged and passes on his title to Sam Wilson.
  • Not much is mentioned about Hawkeye after Endgame but he is deffinately still alive.
  • Black Widow dies when her and Hawkeye set out to retrieve the Soul Stone during Endgame. A sacrifice of a soul is needed in order to obtain the Stone. Black Widow and Hawkeye fight over sacrificing themselves and she eventually wins leading to her death.
